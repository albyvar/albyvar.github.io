iOS 10 beta 2

Apple released the 2nd beta of iOS 10 to developers. Now it's possible to unlock the device by just tapping on the home button for devices with touch id. Also, it's possible to download funny emojis directly from the messages app. New icons on quick options in control center with 3d touch. Download the profile to install the beta just now!
